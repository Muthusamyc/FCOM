We can collect your garments in three modes as given below. However, our vision is to provide doorstep services.

(a) Home Visit : On FCOM website, you will either Request Call Back or book an order by clicking on Book Now. You pick items for your cart and while checking out, under Service Mode, you choose Home Visit. Our representative will contact you to schedule a slot. On the scheduled date, our team visits you, takes measurements, suggests on designs and solutions, raises the order and picks the fabric for stitching or garments for alterations.

(b) Store Visit : On FCOM website, you will either Request Call Back or book an order by clicking on Book Now. You pick items for your cart and while checking out, under Service Mode, you choose Store Visit. Our representative will contact you to guide you on how to reach our store which is at Indiranagar, Bangalore. You can find us on google maps. We are open from Monday – Saturday between 9:30am to 7pm. On your visit, our team, takes measurements, suggests on designs and solutions, raises the order and picks the fabric for stitching or garments for alterations.

(c) Send thru other services : On FCOM website, you will either Request Call Back or book an order by clicking on Book Now. You pick items for your cart and while checking out, under Service Mode, you choose Send thru other services. We are open from Monday – Saturday between 9:30am to 7pm. On receipt of your fabric or garments, our team contacts you for measurements and other details, suggests on designs and solutions, raises the order.
